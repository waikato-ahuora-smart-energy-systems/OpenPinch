Decision Workflows
==================

Choose a tutorial by the engineering decision:

Establish the thermal baseline
   Start with notebooks 01 and 02 for complete and focused heat integration.

Represent variable heat capacity or phase change
   Use notebook 03 for multi-segment stream modelling.

Compare retrofit assumptions
   Use notebooks 04 and 05 for named scenarios, batches, and persistence.

Assess operating periods
   Use notebooks 06, 10, 13, and 17 for multiperiod heat integration, HPR,
   cogeneration, and HEN synthesis.

Screen HPR and MVR
   Use notebooks 08 through 11.

Assess cogeneration or site energy transfer
   Use notebooks 12 through 14.

Synthesize a network
   Use notebooks 15 through 17 with the HEN solver extras.

Publish results
   Use notebook 18 for cached observation and explicit export/dashboard calls.

See :doc:`tutorial-coverage-map` for the exact operation mapping.
